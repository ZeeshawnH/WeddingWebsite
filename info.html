  • Hadith-e-Kisa

    The Tradition of the Cloak

    Recited by Kamran Mirza

    Hadith-e-Kisa (linked here), also known as the Tradition of the Cloak, is a famous narration in Islamic tradition that recounts a significant event involving the Prophet Muhammad (SAWS), his daughter Fatima (SA), his cousin and son-in-law Ali (AS), and his grandsons Hasan (AS) and Hussain (AS).


    The narration describes an incident where the Prophet Muhammad (SAWS) gathered his family members under a cloak (kisa). As they gathered beneath it, the Prophet Muhammad supplicated to Allah (SWT), invoking blessings and seeking the purification and protection of his family members.


    The narration highlights the spiritual significance and purity of the Prophet Muhammad's (SAWS) family, emphasizing their status as the Ahl al-Bayt (People of the House) who hold a special place in Islam. It serves as a reminder of their sanctity and the importance of following their teachings and example.
